I've always wanted to learn coding! Over the past semester, I was able to take a web design class in the graphic design department. Followings are selected course works of mine.

01. Traces of Me

"Choose a span of time, record or gather data that you need, and create a multi-page website that represents data and time."

Concept

Campus buildings requiring Student ID cards to gain access makes it seem like my acitivities are under surveillance, but on the other hand, activities without ID card swipes are almost a mystery, which I find super intersting! I made this website out of curiosity that how much a viewer can puzzle up with only data from my ID card swipe.

02. Top of the World

"Create a website that expresses a song."

Concept

The song that I've chosen is "Top of the World" by The Carpenters. The lyrics is about how 'love' brings joy to life and makes the smallest things extraordinary. My interpretation is that viewer's interaction with the website generates color. And as colors show, the page becomes more and more lively, just like a spirit being nourished by love.
