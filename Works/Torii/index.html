Torii

Furniture Design / 2019

A Japanese temple inspired table for the home. Torii is made of both light and dark colored wood that add contrast and character to the furniture. This was a solid wood frame construction project, aiming to see how form and structure can complement one another.

Torii has both angular and rounded edges
to give it some playfulness.

All pieces running on the X and Y axis
gives Torii more structural strength.
